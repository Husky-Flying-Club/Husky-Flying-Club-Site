Husky Flying Club offers 3 F/A-18 Virtual Reality Flight Simulators on the top floor of Clark Hall. Members are able to request 1-on-1 flight instruction with our Simulator Instructors.

Operation: Coast-2-Coast is the first Joint-Detachment Virtual Pilot Training program teaching both Cadets and University students Basic Fighter Maneuvers. Members participate in maneuver training, dogfighting competitions, and large-scale Campaigns. The program is nation wide so if you are interested, join our discord to sign up!
